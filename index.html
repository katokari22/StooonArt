Welcome...

Stooon Art, founded by Sami Abou El Faouz on October 13, 2023, is a unique clothing brand that seamlessly blends the richness of Moroccan heritage with contemporary fashion trends. Our creations are not just garments, but wearable art pieces that narrate a captivating story. Our brand is rooted in the belief of celebrating individuality and the enchantment of cultural diversity. Each design we create is a vibrant homage to the dynamic colors, elaborate patterns, and ageless techniques intrinsic to Moroccan artistry. At Stooon Art, we’re not just designing clothes - we’re curating a fashion experience. We invite you to embark on this sartorial journey with us as we infuse your wardrobe with a touch of Moroccan charm. Stay tuned for our forthcoming collection!
